## Overview

Many developers use flat-rate subscriptions like Claude Max ($100/mo) or OpenAI Pro ($200/mo) without knowing if they're actually saving money.

CostHawk tracks your usage and calculates what you would have paid at retail rates, showing you the real ROI of your subscriptions.

## How It Works

1. **Track Usage** - CostHawk monitors your API calls and token usage
2. **Calculate Retail Cost** - We apply current retail pricing to your usage
3. **Compare** - See subscription cost vs retail cost
4. **Show Savings** - Instant visibility into whether you're saving money

## Savings Status

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Saving | Your retail cost exceeds your subscription - you're saving money |
| Below Breakeven | You're not using enough to justify the subscription cost |
| No Usage | No tracked usage for this period |
